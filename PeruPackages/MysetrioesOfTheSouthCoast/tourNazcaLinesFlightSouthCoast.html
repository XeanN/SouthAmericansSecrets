Fly over the enigmatic Nazca Lines, contemplate the rugged beauty of Paracas National Reserve, marvel at the abundant marine life at Islas Ballestas, and sample some of the best pisco wine in the Ica region. Relax by a quaint oasis in the desert after an exhilarating dune buggy ride over the mountainous dunes. Your stay in Paracas will be at the charming Santa Maria Hotel, which offers clean, modern rooms with private bathrooms, hot water, cable television, internet access at reception, and a continental breakfast.
