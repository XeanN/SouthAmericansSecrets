Detail

The best introduction to Peruvian natural history and its three historical periods: pre-Hispanic, colonial, and contemporary. Lima was home to pre-Columbian cultures dating back thousands of years. The land of the Incas, Lima was conquered by Spaniards on January 18, 1535. Tour with South Americans' Secrets to retrace the steps of this millennial history. Discover ancestral Lima's "Huaca Pucllana" – the Incas' most important ceremonial and administrative center and South America's most culturally developed civilization between 200 AD and 700 AD. Marvel at the adobe clay pyramid constructed during this period with its seven staggering platforms.
