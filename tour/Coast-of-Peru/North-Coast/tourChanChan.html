We meet at our office and escort you to the Paracas dock, where you will embark on a two-hour round-trip tour of Peru's most famous islands. This excursion offers breathtaking views of magnificent rock formations sculpted by nature and is home to Humboldt penguins, South American sea lions, and a wide variety of marine birds.
