The Pampas Galeras National Reserve, established in 1967, spans 6,500 hectares, with an influence over 60,000 hectares and several communities. It promotes community development through sustainable resource management. The International Festival of the Vicuña revives Andean culture, utilizing Pre-Inca techniques to manage these animals. Participants create a human fence using music and movement to guide the vicuñas into a corral for classification and shearing, after which they are released safely. This initiative involves farmers, students, professionals, and the public. Our guided tour begins when your guide picks you up from your hotel, located about 90 km from Nazca.
