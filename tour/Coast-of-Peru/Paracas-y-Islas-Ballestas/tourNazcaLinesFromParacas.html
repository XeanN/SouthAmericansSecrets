Nazca, located on the southern coast of Peru, is also the name of a Pre-Inca culture that flourished in the region between 200 BC and 800 AD. The Nazca culture is renowned for its massive and enigmatic geoglyphs, which are impossible to view from the ground. Without the technology of flight, it would have been impossible for them to appreciate their own creations. Your guides will share various intriguing theories about why the Nazca people invested such immense effort in creating these lines. Our guided program begins with a pickup from your hotel. We use Caravans as our aircraft, accommodating up to 12 passengers. Our English-speaking pilots will explain each mysterious figure etched into the landscape below.
