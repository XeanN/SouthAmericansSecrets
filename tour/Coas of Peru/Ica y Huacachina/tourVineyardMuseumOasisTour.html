The region is Peru's wine capital and the world’s Pisco hub, home to the country's largest wineries—both traditional and industrial. Tours often include renowned spots like El Catador and Caravedo. Just 5 km southwest of Ica lies Huacachina, a picturesque tourist village encircling a natural oasis. It’s a popular retreat for travelers, offering relaxation and some of the best sandboarding experiences in the world. For those not keen on extreme sports, a dune buggy ride through the immense surrounding dunes provides breathtaking views and is an absolute must when visiting the area.
