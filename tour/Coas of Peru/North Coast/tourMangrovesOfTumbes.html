Mangroves of Tumbes

The National Mangrove Sanctuary is protected by SERNANP to prevent contamination, deforestation, and to preserve local traditions.

Experience Tumbes: Explore lush mangroves, savor local cuisine, and meet fascinating wildlife!/p>

12 hours aprox
100$ per person
Minimum 2 participants

Detail

The Tumbes mangroves (Manglares de Tumbes) serve as a refuge for the vulnerable American Crocodile, as well as many other species of fish, mammals, and birds. Traveling through the canals by canoe or kayak provides visitors with a close-up experience of this natural wonder. Afterward, enjoy lunch in the dry forest, followed by a visit to the picturesque fishing village of Puerto Pizarro, where the Peruvian Ministry of Fisheries maintains a zoo that protects migratory birds and over 300 crocodiles. The mangroves and the village of Puerto Pizarro are excellent places to visit year-round.

Include:

  • Pick up from your hotel in Los Órganos, Máncora, or Punta Sal
  • Private transportation
  • Entrance fees included
  • Lunch in the dry forest
  • Professional English-speaking guide

Not Include:

  • Extras & gratuities.
Price: $100
