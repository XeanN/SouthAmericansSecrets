Snorkel with Turtles

El Nuro, a small fishing village in northern Peru, is home to an EcOceánica conservation program for sea turtles.

Snorkel with thriving sea turtles at El Ñuro's protected waters!

2 hours aprox
$35 per person
Minimum 2 participants

Detail

EcOceánica is a Peruvian non-profit organization dedicated to marine conservation. Their efforts have significantly increased the population of sea turtles (Peru is home to 5 of the world's 7 species), with El Ñuro being one of their greatest successes. Local fishermen, encouraged by EcOceánica, have agreed to stop catching the turtles. Now, fishing bycatch is thrown to the turtles, helping boost their population and encouraging them to gather near the sea docks, where some of the best snorkeling can be found.

Include:

  • Pick up from your hotel in Máncora, Los Órganos or Punta Sal
  • Private transportation
  • Dock entrance fees
  • Professional English-speaking guide

Not Include:

  • Extras & gratuities.
