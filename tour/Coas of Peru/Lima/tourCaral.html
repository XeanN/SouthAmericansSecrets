For those interested in archaeology and adventure, this tour departs from Lima via the Ruta del Sol (approximately 2 hours) to explore the Norte Central civilization, also known as Caral or Caral-Supe. This complex Pre-Columbian society encompassed nearly 30 major population centers in the Norte Chico region of north-central coastal Peru. Caral, the oldest known civilization in the Americas, flourished between 3000 BC and 1800 BC and is one of six ancient sites where civilization independently emerged. The name Caral-Supe derives from Caral in the Supe Valley, a key site in Norte Chico. This society developed about a millennium after Sumer in Mesopotamia, around the same time as the pyramids of Ancient Egypt, and predates the Mesoamerican Olmec by nearly two millennia.
