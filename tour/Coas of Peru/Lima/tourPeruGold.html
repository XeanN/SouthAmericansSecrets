Peruvian Gold

Explore Peru's stunning gold collection and the remarkable Arms of the World Collection.

Discover ancient Peru's breathtaking gold artifacts, showcasing master metalworking techniques over 3,000 years old!

Detail

The "cire perdue," or lost wax process, known across the Orient and lost in the West until the Renaissance, was the most common method they used on our tour of Peru’s Gold Museum, visitors will admire an awe-inspiring collection of gold artifacts from various pre-Columbian Civilizations, some dating back over 3,000 years.
