Pachacamac

A unique archaeological and mystical experience

Explore Pachacamac: ancient citadel, pyramidal temples, and sacred artifacts.

12 hours approx
Departs daily at 6.00am
$45 per person
Minimum 2 participants

Detail

Embark on a guided tour of Pachacamac, an ancient ceremonial citadel revered by Andean civilizations. The site features pyramidal temples built by pre-Columbian cultures and the Incas. Highlights include the Temple of Pachacamac, the Sun God Shrine, and the Palace of the Virgins of the Sun. At the museum, see an idol of the deity Pachacamac, once visible only to high priests who interpreted the divine oracle. A mystical and sacred site, Pachacamac was believed to be part of the world’s spiritual axis.

Include:

  • Private VIP transport to Pisco airport from your hotel in Lima (3 hours)
  • VIP First Class Guided tour of the "Candelbra" geoglyph and the Ballestas Islands (2 hours)
  • Overflight of Nazca Lines (95 minutes)
  • Private VIP transport return to Lima
  • All transport, fees and taxes
  • English-speaking guide.

Not Include:

  • Extras & gratuities.
