Cruise Member Islas Ballestas Standard Tour

Explore stunning islands and wildlife in Peru's breathtaking Paracas National Reserve.

The Islas Ballestas are Paracas's top wildlife attraction, featuring unique bird and marine species.

5-6 hours aprox
85$ per person
Minimum 2 participants

Detail

We pick you up from the port and escort you to Paracas dock for a round-trip tour of Peru's famous islands. Enjoy stunning rock formations, home to Humboldt penguins, South American sea lions, and diverse marine birds. Our yacht tours include a certified crew and are fully insured. Next, we explore the Paracas National Reserve, a natural area with 224 bird species, 180 fish species, and 20 cetaceans. It features 114 archaeological sites, showcasing the ancient Paracas civilization's relationship with the sea. Declared a Ramsar Site in 1991, the reserve offers breathtaking views, white sand beaches, and vibrant sedimentary rocks.

Include:

  • Maximum of 28 participants
  • All entrance fees included
  • Harbor tax included
  • Personal insurance for injuries
  • Pickup and drop-off directly from the port/harbor
  • All transportation provided.
  • English-speaking guide.

Not Include:

  • Extras & gratuities.
